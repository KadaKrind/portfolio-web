Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CloudSync

What is CloudSync?

CloudSync is a modern project management platform designed to help teams collaborate, track progress, and deliver projects on time. It combines task management, team collaboration, and advanced analytics in one easy-to-use interface.

Is there a free plan?

Yes! CloudSync offers a free plan that includes up to 5 projects and 5 team members. It's perfect for getting started and testing the platform before upgrading to a paid plan.

Can I import projects from other tools?

Yes, we support importing from popular tools like Asana, Monday.com, and Jira. Our import wizard makes it easy to migrate your projects with all their data intact.

How is my data secured?

We use bank-level encryption (TLS 1.2+) for data in transit and AES-256 for data at rest. We're SOC2 Type II certified and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations.

Do you offer a free trial?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial of any paid plan. No credit card required - just sign up and start exploring all the features CloudSync has to offer.

What integrations do you support?

We support integrations with 100+ popular tools including Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, Jira, Asana, and many more. Check our integration marketplace to see the full list.
